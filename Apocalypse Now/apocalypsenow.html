Apocalypse Now is an American epic adventure war film in 1979, during the Vietnam War. Produced and directed by Francis Ford Coppola and starring Martin Sheen, Marlon Brando, and Robert Duvall, the film follows the central character, Captain Benjamin L. Willard (Martin Sheen), on a secret mission to assassinate the renegade and presumed insane Colonel Walter E. Kurtz (Brando) This film does touch upon sensitive topic as the Vietnam war was significant event in the history of the world and the international relationships between Vietnam, China and America. This film shows horror and psychological trauma of war. The gore depicted in this movie makes the film unsuitable for any viewers under 18.
